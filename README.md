# AI Job Market Impact Analysis (2024–2030)

## 🔍 Overview
This project provides a comprehensive exploratory data analysis (EDA) into the projected influence of AI on global job markets through 2030. As AI reshapes the professional landscape, understanding how different industries, education levels, and locations will be impacted is critical. 

## 🛠 Tech Stack
* **Python**: Primary language for data processing.
* **Pandas**: Used for data cleaning and filtering.
* **NumPy**: Used for efficient numerical aggregation.
* **Seaborn & Matplotlib**: Used to build visualizations.

## 📊 Key Findings
* **Industry Resilience**: Analyzed sectors with the highest job growth.
* **Educational ROI**: Investigated how AI impact levels correlate with median salaries.
* **Geographic Hubs**: Identified innovation hotspots for high-impact AI roles.

## 📈 Visual Highlights
* **Salary Benchmarks**: Comparison of compensation across sectors.
* **AI Impact Distribution**: Pie chart of AI integration across 8 industries vs countries.
* **Growth Projections**: Grouped bar chart of job openings (2024-2030).

## 💡 Strategic Takeaways
* **AI as an Enabler**: Industries with "High" AI impact are showing high projected job growth.
* **The Geography of Innovation**: High-impact roles are concentrated in specific hubs.
* **Skill-Based Compensation**: Professional skills are becoming more valuable than traditional credentials.
